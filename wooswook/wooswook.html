School Project

Overview

Brief: A platform for students and restaurants to work simply and quickly.

My role: UX design, UX research, UI design, visual design, mock up, video producing.

Problems: As the aim of student cafeteria is to provide a place for students to have their meal more convenient and affordable. However, in our school, ever when lunch time, cafeteria is full of workers that work near our university and it take a long time for students to queue in line.

Solution: We come up with an idea to deal with this situation. Designing an app for students to order their meals before going to the cafeteria. Then, restaurants can start preparing. It can cut down the waiting time and students don’t need to queue with other workers.

Research

After we observing this situation and interview some people, we list some potential reasons that may made the cafeteria crowded.

Long Queue


There are several reasons for a long line. Firstly, every time a person order a meal clerk needs to write it down. Then, they would go through the paying process. After that, clerk give the order to kitchen.

Waiting


After ordering, customers still need to wait for their food. Hence, there would be many people standing in the cafeteria no matter they are eating here or not.

Finding Seat


It is hard to find a seat at lunch time, so there would be many people walking around looking for seats. Furthermore, as we observe, there are a lot of people that are not student having meals. This makes students much harder to have lunch in cafeteria.

Brainstorming

we tried to figure out how to create something that is special for students not all people in the dining hall.

Student ID Number


We initially found out that every student have their own ID number, therefore, ID number is required to login.

Order Beforehand


Providing students an opportunity to book what they want to have and select the pick-up time they prefer. So, students don’t need to spend time on queuing and waiting for meal.

We want to simplify the whole order process to make it faster and easier. So we not only provide students a solution but also take care of the whole system. Design a POS system for restaurants to operate.

Smart Pay


Cash payment takes time because people need to take out their wallet and wait for change. As our student cards are also an easy card(contactless smartcard system in Taiwan, for payment on public transport services and some stores), we connect the easy card payment with our app. So students can simply checkout with their smartphone rather than finding change in their wallets.

POS System


Ordering and checkout process take time and make the queue longer. We design a POS system to make the process simpler. Then it can save time from writing down the order and give it to kitchen. Moreover, it also provide restaurant to view their revenue.
