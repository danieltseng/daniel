KNOCK KNOCK

Oct 2017 - Dec 2017

School Project

Overview

Brief: An APP for language learning, chatting with foreigners and teaching others your in language.

My role: UX design, UX research, UI design, visual design, mock up, prototype.

Problems: Speaking foreign languages is an essential ability to connect the rest of the world. However, there are no much opportunities for people to practice or use in daily life.

Solution: The best way to improve language ability is to use it as often as possible. We designed a platform for our users to practice foreign languages by matching them with local people in a random chat. Also, our users can also watch tutorial videos on it.

User Research

We interviewed five people who were learning a foreign language and another five people who with high level foreign language ability to understand how was their language learning situation. We separate those results into 2 categories.

Practice


If there is a partner that can learn foreign language together or even a local person to practice with, it would be helpful. Watching videos and listen to music would also help.

Those five participates with high level foreign language ability use the foreign language everyday.

Learn


8 participants learned in school and 2 by themselves. Most of them liked online resource, such as online tutor and Youtube. These are free or in a lower price than learning in school.

Brainstorming

We took the main features from user research and category the problems. Then we used pen and paper to rapidly brainstorm possible ideas.

After several rounds of meeting and ideation, we came out with the idea of combining “learning” and “making friends”. As the popularity of dating APPs, random pairing can also use on practicing target language with others.

Random Chat


Users can choose the target language then have a random chat with someone who is good at it.

Video


Users can learn from the tutorial other users uploaded and user can also up load their own tutorials.

Flow Chart

Focus on two main points Practicing and Learning, only set three tabs for the APP, including match, course and profile. Match is for the random pairing and also including friends. Course is for the tutorials and upload. The first page users would see after they sign in is the Match page since it is the main function of our APP.
